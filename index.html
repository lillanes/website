Research

My interests are in the areas of Artificial Intelligence Search and Automated Planning. I've done some research on search under real-time constraints, and I'm currently interested in the application of abstraction techniques to AI Planning.

Recently I've had nascent interests in Model Checking, Software Verification and Program Synthesis, and in how ideas and technologies developed for these areas can be applied on search problems from Artificial Intelligence and viceversa.

Academic Bio

I've been a PhD student at the University of Toronto since 2014. Before that, I did my undergrad in Computer Engineering and MSc in Computer Science at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile. My master's degree was supervised by Jorge Baier.
